Our Story

Shawn Gallagher Landscaping was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality landscaping services to homeowners and businesses throughout Schuylkill and Berks counties.

What began as a small operation with just one truck and a few basic tools has grown into a full-service landscaping company with a team of skilled professionals and state-of-the-art equipment.

Owner Shawn Gallagher grew up in Schuylkill County and has always had a passion for the outdoors and landscaping. After earning a degree in Horticulture from Pennsylvania State University and gaining experience working for established landscaping companies, Shawn decided to start his own business to serve the community he calls home.

Today, Shawn Gallagher Landscaping is known throughout the region for our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and exceptional customer service. We take pride in transforming ordinary outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional landscapes that our clients can enjoy for years to come.
